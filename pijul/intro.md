## Yet, what is Pijul?

- An independently developed **V**ersion **C**ontrol **S**ystem
- Built upon a **patch based** approach to change tracking (as opposed to *snapshot based* VCS)
- written in Rust
- Homepage: [pijul.org](https://pijul.org)
